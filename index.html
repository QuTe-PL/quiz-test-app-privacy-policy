Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 21, 2026

Thank you for using QuizTest. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

Information We Collect

QuizTest uses Firebase Crashlytics, a crash-reporting service provided by Google, to help us identify and fix stability issues. When a crash occurs, Crashlytics automatically collects the following data:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device model, operating system version, and device orientation
  • Crashlytics installation UUID (a random identifier tied to the app installation, not to you personally)
  • RAM and disk usage at the time of the crash

This list may not be exhaustive. For a full list of data collected by Firebase Crashlytics, please refer to the Third-Party Services section below.

QuizTest also uses Google Analytics 4 (Firebase Analytics), an app-analytics service provided by Google, to help us understand how the app is used and improve the user experience. Firebase Analytics automatically collects the following data:

  • App usage data and events (e.g., screens viewed, features used)
  • Device information (model, operating system version, screen size)
  • App version and installation source
  • Anonymous/pseudonymous identifiers (Firebase instance ID, app-instance ID)
  • Country/region (coarse location derived from IP address — the IP address itself is not stored by Google Analytics 4)

This list may not be exhaustive. For a full list of data collected by Firebase Analytics, please refer to the Third-Party Services section below.

We do not collect any directly identifying personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. The data collected through Crashlytics and Analytics is limited to pseudonymous, technical, and usage information as described above.

Your Choices

When you first open QuizTest, you will be asked whether you consent to data collection for analytics and crash reporting. You can decline at that time, and no data will be collected.

If you change your mind later, you can update your preference at any time in the app's settings. Disabling data collection will stop Firebase Crashlytics and Google Analytics 4 from collecting any further data from your device.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates Firebase Crashlytics and Google Analytics 4 (Firebase Analytics) by Google. Data collected by these services is sent to and processed on Google's servers.

For a complete description of the data collected by these services and how Google processes it, please refer to:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics Privacy & Data Practices

Children's Privacy

The data collected by Firebase Crashlytics and Google Analytics 4 is pseudonymous and does not contain any personally identifiable information. Our app does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
