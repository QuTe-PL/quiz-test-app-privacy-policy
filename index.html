Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

Thank you for using QuizTest. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

Information We Collect

QuizTest uses Firebase Crashlytics, a crash-reporting service provided by Google, to help us identify and fix stability issues. When a crash occurs, Crashlytics automatically collects the following data:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device model, operating system version, and device orientation
  • Crashlytics installation UUID (a random identifier tied to the app installation, not to you personally)
  • RAM and disk usage at the time of the crash

This data is used solely for the purpose of improving app stability and fixing bugs. We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or advertising identifiers.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates Firebase Crashlytics by Google. Crash data collected by Crashlytics is sent to and processed on Google's servers. No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.

You can review Google's privacy practices for Firebase at: Firebase Privacy.

Children's Privacy

The crash data collected by Firebase Crashlytics does not contain any personally identifiable information. Our app does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
