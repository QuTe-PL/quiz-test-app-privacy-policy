Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 22, 2026

Thank you for using QuizTest. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information whatsoever. The app operates entirely on your device and does not communicate with any server or third-party service.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports sent off-device
  • Any other information that could be used to identify you

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Because we collect no data from any user, our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
